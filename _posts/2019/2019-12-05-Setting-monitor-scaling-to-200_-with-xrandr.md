You are just missing the `--scale 2x2` argument. So use:

``` 
xrandr \
  --output DP-4 --primary --pos 0x0 --scale 2x2 \
  --output DP-2 --pos 3840x0 --scale 2x2 \
  --output eDP-1-1 --off
```

**Note:** Double quoting the monitor and position is unnecessary so I removed the `"`.
